Why is compost good for the environment?

  • Proper disposal of organic wastes (Beneficial to the environment)
  • Allows for recycling of nutrients
  • More money saved because less maintenance and care is needed for crops
  • The agricultural sector highly depends on composting
  • Less effort needed because not much watering needs to be done.
  • Edible crops are healthier for consumption because chemical fertilisers are not used.
