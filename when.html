When is compost created?

  • Sir Albert Howard, father of the organic method. Between 1905 and 1934 he devised the Indore method of compost making.
  • The first "written" account of compost making is on a set of clay tablets carved during the reign of King Sargon during the Akkadian Dynasty, which lasted about 160 years between 2320 BC and 2120 BC.

When will compost be ready?

Compost can be made in as little as six to eight weeks, or, more usually, it can take a year or more. In general, the more effort you put in, the quicker you will get compost. When the ingredients you have put in your container have turned into a dark brown, earthy-smelling material, the composting process is complete.
